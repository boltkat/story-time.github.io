The greatest hero

A Superpower is an exceptional or extraordinary power or ability. Powers can be obtained in 2 ways. The first way is through a great act of heroism, The greater the act the better the ability will be. Your power can change or level up if you complete and even greater action or a bunch of small act's which will slowly increase your level.

The second way is to steal an ability from another. However, to completely steal the ability of another, you must kill the original owner of the ability. That does not guarantee that you will be able to use the ability.

Every person has what is known as a frame. This frame is used to judge the level of your ability. A bigger frame would allow for more power and a wider range to use your superpower. When using your ability the frame will slowly shrink and become smaller. If your ability becomes too small, you won't be able to use your ability, for a certain amount of time. Some people even lose their power or die from contact to earth energy which was one hundred times more strong than the moon energy which was only able to create a barrier. Due to the frame, People who steal an ability larger than the range of their frame will not be able to control the power, as it is more than their body can take.
